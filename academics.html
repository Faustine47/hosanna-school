School Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to cater to diverse academic interests, preparing students for higher education and the workforce.

  • Science Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Mathematics and Computing: Mathematics, Computer Science
  • Languages: English, French, Kiswahili

Academic Departments

Our school has dedicated departments ensuring quality teaching:

  • Science Department: Provides hands-on experiments and research programs.
  • Mathematics Department: Strengthens problem-solving and quantitative reasoning.
  • Languages Department: Offers linguistic and literary studies for effective communication.

Co-Curricular Activities

We encourage students to participate in extracurricular activities to build leadership and teamwork skills.

  • Sports: Football, Basketball, Swimming
  • Clubs: Debate Club, MDD Club, Science Club, Chess Club
  • Music & Arts: Choir, Traditional Dance, Fine Arts
